Site Purpose:

To update citizens of Orlando, Florida on the current weather and the weather to come in the next week. It will show 7 days of weather with the current weather showing at the top. It's purpose will be to prepare those who are inside at home wanting to go outside how they should dress and what to expect with the weather.
