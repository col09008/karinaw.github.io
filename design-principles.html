Design Principles Document

Karina Weaver

White Space and Clean Design

  • Company Name: Type Form
  • Website: Type Form
  • This website represent White Space by not filling the entire page and letting the space speak for itself. It helps let the content breathe by not having it all be smashed. White space can be get boring when done wrong.

Alignment

  • Company Name: IGN
  • Website: IGN Store
  • This website represent alignment by having all of the clothing aligned for the viewer to see better. It keeps it organized and easier to browse through their product. It keeps it organized as well and makes it pleasing to the eye.

Repetition

  • Company Name: Weaver Ad Partners
  • Website: Weaver Ad Partners
  • This website represent repetition by having the same text and colors all throughout the page. The consistency with the background all the way down while you scroll keeps it smooth and easy for the eye to follow.
